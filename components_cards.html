Cards

A card is a flexible content container, and gives a user a quick overview of secondary content, or high level information on a primary subject.

Usage

Cards should be used in the context of a gallery or providing users an overview of content in a digestable overview.


Accessibility

Cards should always utilize the appropriate HTML elements, including subheadlines.


Examples

Single Card

A single card should always have an illustrative or featured image, a title, a description and a CTA.
