Typography

The Magpie design system utilizes two primary fonts: Adonis & Halyard Book

Adonis is a serif typface designed by Natalia Vasilyeva for Paratype. Paratype type foundry has been designing and distrubting web fonts since the 1980's and is home to many of the web's most popular fonts, such as PT Sans and Futura.

Halyard belongs to a family of 'Grotesque' sans serif typefaces designed by Joshua Dardenfor Darden Studio. Darden Studio describes Haylard as at 'once familiar and pleasingly distinctive.'
Magpie employs 'Halyard Display Book' for its lightweight feel.
